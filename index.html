The Minotaur: This creature is said to have the head of a bull and the body of a man. It is said to live in labyrinths deep in the earth, and can only be defeated by someone who can solve the riddles that lead to it.

The Phoenix: This mythical bird is said to be reborn from its own ashes. It is a symbol of hope and rebirth, and is said to live for hundreds of years.

The Unicorn: This mythical creature is said to be a pure white horse with a single horn on its forehead. It is said to have healing powers, and its horn is said to be able to purify water.

The Dragons: These mythical creatures come in many different forms, but all are said to be powerful and feared by humans. They are often associated with fire, and some cultures believe that they can control the weather.
